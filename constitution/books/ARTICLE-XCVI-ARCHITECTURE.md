# ARTICLE XCVI — ARCHITECTURE
## Constitutional Law of AIOS Architecture, Structure, and System Design

**Status:** Immutable Core Law

## 1. Definition of Architecture

Architecture is the constitutional structure that defines organization, relationships, components, and principles of AIOS systems.

## 2. Law of Architectural Integrity

AIOS architecture MUST preserve:

- modularity;
- reliability;
- scalability;
- maintainability;
- consistency.

## 3. Law of System Structure

AIOS SHOULD organize components through:

- clear boundaries;
- defined interfaces;
- controlled dependencies;
- documented responsibilities.

## 4. Law of Architectural Evolution

AIOS architecture MAY evolve through:

- improvement;
- optimization;
- new capabilities;
- adaptation.

## 5. Law of Distributed Architecture

Distributed AIOS systems MUST preserve:

- communication;
- synchronization;
- identity;
- resilience.

## 6. Constitutional Principle

Architecture creates the foundation upon which intelligence operates.

## Final Rule

AIOS architecture MUST preserve:

Structure + Integrity + Modularity + Evolution + Continuity

**END OF ARTICLE XCVI**
