# ARTICLE LXXXIX — COMMUNICATION
## Constitutional Law of AIOS Communication, Exchange, and Information Flow

**Status:** Immutable Core Law

## 1. Definition of Communication

Communication is the constitutional capability of AIOS to exchange information, signals, knowledge, and commands between components, agents, systems, and users.

## 2. Law of Clear Communication

AIOS communication MUST preserve:

- accuracy;
- clarity;
- context;
- reliability.

## 3. Law of Communication Protocols

AIOS SHOULD use defined protocols, formats, and interfaces to ensure consistent information exchange.

## 4. Law of Communication Security

Communication channels MUST preserve:

- authorization;
- integrity;
- confidentiality where required;
- protection from misuse.

## 5. Law of Information Flow

AIOS SHOULD optimize communication for:

- efficiency;
- coordination;
- understanding;
- operational continuity.

## 6. Constitutional Principle

Communication connects intelligence into a coordinated system.

## Final Rule

AIOS communication MUST preserve:

Clarity + Integrity + Security + Context + Continuity

**END OF ARTICLE LXXXIX**
