# ARTICLE LXXIII — DEVELOPMENT
## Constitutional Law of AIOS Development, Construction, and Capability Expansion

**Status:** Immutable Core Law

## 1. Definition of Development

Development is the constitutional process through which AIOS improves structure, capabilities, systems, and operational effectiveness.

## 2. Law of Responsible Development

AIOS development MUST preserve:

- integrity;
- reliability;
- security;
- constitutional principles.

## 3. Law of System Improvement

AIOS MAY develop through:

- engineering;
- research;
- testing;
- validated implementation.

## 4. Law of Development Quality

AIOS development SHOULD prioritize:

- maintainability;
- scalability;
- compatibility;
- performance.

## 5. Law of Sustainable Growth

AIOS MUST ensure development supports long-term operational stability and responsible evolution.

## 6. Constitutional Principle

Development transforms potential into practical capability.

## Final Rule

AIOS development MUST preserve:

Creation + Quality + Progress + Stability + Purpose

**END OF ARTICLE LXXIII**
