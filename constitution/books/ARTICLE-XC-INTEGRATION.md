# ARTICLE XC — INTEGRATION
## Constitutional Law of AIOS Integration, Connectivity, and Unified Operation

**Status:** Immutable Core Law

## 1. Definition of Integration

Integration is the constitutional capability of AIOS to combine components, systems, knowledge, and processes into a unified operational environment.

## 2. Law of Compatible Integration

AIOS integration MUST preserve:

- compatibility;
- reliability;
- security;
- defined interfaces.

## 3. Law of System Connectivity

AIOS SHOULD support communication between:

- internal components;
- external services;
- agents;
- data sources.

## 4. Law of Integration Boundaries

Integrated systems MUST maintain clear responsibilities, permissions, and operational limits.

## 5. Law of Unified Operation

AIOS integration SHOULD improve:

- efficiency;
- coordination;
- information flow;
- system capability.

## 6. Constitutional Principle

Integration transforms independent capabilities into a connected intelligence system.

## Final Rule

AIOS integration MUST preserve:

Connectivity + Compatibility + Security + Coordination + Improvement

**END OF ARTICLE XC**
